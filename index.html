ABOUT ME

Hey! I am James Djamba, a data loving guy. I could identify myself as a GIS analyst, but that would not do justice to my work. Throughout my career, my official job titles have been city planner, document specialist, graduate assistant, and GIS specialist. These titles have fallen short of identifying me and my skillsets. In all of my positions I have aligned myself as the data, GIS, research, graphic design, and web design guy. I have often found myself spearheading the development of documents, infographics, presentations, websites, and more. With that in mind I can proudly say that I am a data guy. I collect, format, geocode, manage, and present data from the moment it is requested to when it is presented.
